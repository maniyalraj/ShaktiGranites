The Company

SHAKTI GRANITE & SHAKTI STONE was established by Mr.S J PATEL(SHAMALBHAI) back in 1996 and is responsible for revolutionizing the granite mining industry of India by becoming the first company to operate a fully mechanized mine in the country.Mr. SHAMALBHAI have a wide experience of mining & processing plant since 1990.

Mr.Shamalbhai patel & Haresh patel, the owner of SHAKTI GRANITE & SHAKTI STONE is responsible for spearheading the revolution of manufacturing of granite in plant & mining also. SHAKTI GRANITE & SHAKTI STONE is a retail and wholesale company situated in PALANPUR,GUJARAT, catering to the requirements of clients from all over India.

Mr. HARESH PATEL, son of SHAMALBHAI took upon himself the mighty task of developing the factory into the finest and the most well managed FACTORY IN GUJARAT.
